# ASK Phase

## Business Task
Analyze YouTube trending data to identify patterns that drive video performance and engagement.

## Key Stakeholders
- Content Creators
- Digital Marketing Teams
- Brands & Advertisers

## Business Questions
1. Which video categories get the highest views?
2. What factors influence trending videos?
3. Does upload timing affect performance?
4. What drives engagement (likes, comments)?
5. How do titles/tags impact views?
